ingredients:
  - "2 tablespoons brown sugar"
  - "1¼ teaspoon apple pie spice<br>- ¾ teaspoon cinnamon<br>- ¼ teaspoon nutmeg<br>- ¼ teaspoon allspice)"
  - "2 tablespoons butter"
  - "⅓ cup pecans"
  - "1 Granny Smith apple"
  - "8 ounce tube Pillsbury Original crescent rolls"
ingredients_subhead: ""
ingredients_2:
  - ""
preparations:
  - "Chop the pecans."
  - "Slice the apple."
directions:
  - "Preheat the oven to 375°F."
  - "In a small bowl, combine the brown sugar and 1 teaspoon apple pie spice."
  - "Melt the butter and toss apple slices in it."
  - "Arrange crescent roll triangles on a baking sheet."
  - "Evenly distribute the brown sugar mixture onto each triangle."
  - "Sprinkle each triangle evenly with the chopped pecans."
  - "Put a slice of apple on the wide end of each triangle."
  - "Wrap crescent roll dough around each apple."
  - "Brush each crescent roll with remaining butter."
  - "Sprinkle lightly with remaining ¼ teaspoon apple pie spice."
  - "Bake until golden brown (10&nbsp;minutes)."
  - "Cool for 5 minutes before serving."
