ingredients:
  - 2 cups Bisquick mix
  - 1 cup quick-cooking oats
  - ¾ cup brown sugar
  - ½ cup butter
  - 1 cup fruit jam

directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 400°F.
  - Grease a 9x9 baking dish.
  - Mix the Bisquick, oats, and brown sugar together in a large bowl.
  - Cut in the butter until mixture is crumbly.
  - Press half of the crumbly mixture in the baking pan.
  - Spread the jam over the crumble mixture to within ¼ inches of the edges.
  - Top with the remaining crumble mixture.
  - Press gently into the fruit.
  - Bake until light brown (25&nbsp;minutes).
  - Let cool before cutting.
